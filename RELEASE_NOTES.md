# Release Notes - CareSyncOS v1.0.0

**Release Date:** December 12, 2025
**Version:** 1.0.0

We are proud to announce the initial release of **CareSyncOS**, a comprehensive mobile operating system designed specifically for modern clinics and healthcare facilities. CareSyncOS streamlines daily operations, allowing healthcare professionals to focus less on administration and more on patient care.

### Key Capabilities

- **Centralized Dashboard**: Get a real-time snapshot of your clinic's health, including daily collections, patient queues, and upcoming schedules.
- **Patient Management**: Seamlessly register new patients, view detailed medical profiles, and track treatment history in one secure location.
- **Smart Scheduling**: Efficiently manage appointments with an intuitive calendar system that tracks status (Confirmed, Pending, Completed).
- **Financial Control**: comprehensive billing system for treatments and diagnostic tests, alongside expense tracking to monitor clinic outgoings.
- **Diagnostic Integration**: integrated workflow for scheduling and tracking lab tests and results.
- **Staff & Support**: Built-in tools for staff attendance tracking and a dedicated support channel for technical assistance.
